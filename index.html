About

I am a second year PhD student at the University of Glasgow in the Formal Analysis, Theory and Algorithms (FATA) section. My supervisor is Prof. David Manlove.
My research focuses on algorithms, matching problems and graph theory.

I've previously worked as an Engineering Software Developer and a Graduate Software Engineer. Check out my GitHub profile.
